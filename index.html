Who is Chicki Chicken and Squid ?


2008

This company is owned by Jamirulhafiz bin Jamil, a very passionate young man with a strong desire in entrepreneurship. It all started on 2008 where Jamirulhafiz also known as Hafiz opening his fried chicken stall named, O'Jai Fried Chicken. Year by year, his small business grows very well and gaining popularity. He then move to another step where he paired up with his friends and tend to make the business even bigger. That is how he created Chicki Chicken and Squid in the year of 2017.

2017

Chicki Chicken and Squid is a chicken based food company that are now become the local favourite. This company was first located in Taman Medan and now has over 10 branches all over Malaysia. Chicken Chicken and Squid has upgraded to few new manu and as well as their services where they deliver customer's order right in front of the customers doorstep.
